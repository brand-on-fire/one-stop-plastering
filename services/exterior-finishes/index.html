Interior & Exterior Finishes: Professional Plastering and Stucco Services

Experience Excellence in Plastering and Stucco Finishes

When it comes to sprucing up your home or office, you’ll want the best of the best. And what could be better than expertly applied plastering or stucco work to give your interiors and exteriors a touch of class and sophistication? That’s where One Stop Plastering comes in. We’re not just another construction company; we’re your partners in creating spaces that reflect your style, taste, and personality.

 

We specialize in a wide array of interior and exterior finishes that can transform your space from ordinary to extraordinary. Whether you’re thinking of a classic, smooth plaster finish or a rustic, textured stucco, our team of professionals has got you covered. With years of experience under our belts, we’ve got the skills, knowledge, and passion to deliver results that are nothing short of spectacular.

Interior Finishes

Whether you’re renovating a home or building a new commercial property, it’s essential to have an experts, such as One Stop Plastering, handle the interior finishes. Our skilled team will work closely with you to determine the best solution to fit your unique project requirements. We offer various plastering options for your interior spaces, including the following techniques:

Smooth Trowel

This technique creates a smooth, clean finish perfect for modern designs or classic aesthetics.

Sand Swirl

Add character to your walls with this textured finish that features elegant swirling patterns in the plaster.

Custom Textures

Have something specific in mind? Our team can create custom textures suited to your preferences and design style.

Exterior Finishes

A building’s exterior is just as important as the interior, serving as the first impression to your clients or guests. At One Stop Plastering, we provide professional stucco and plaster services for exterior walls, ensuring your property looks its best. Our comprehensive exterior finishing services include:

Stucco Applications

Stucco is a versatile material that offers durability, energy efficiency, and fire resistance. We provide a variety of stucco finishes, including traditional three-coat, one-coat, and EIFS (Exterior Insulation and Finish System). Our team will guide you to the best option for your project needs.

Architectural Foam Details

Customize your property’s exterior with stunning architectural foam details. Available in various shapes and sizes, foam details can enhance your building’s design and add considerable curb appeal.
