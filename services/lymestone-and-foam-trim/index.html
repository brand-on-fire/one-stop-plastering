Enhance Your Home with Our Durable Lymestone & Foam Trim Services

What is Lymestone?

lymestone is an eco-friendly, natural limestone-based product that is designed to replicate the look and feel of authentic stone surfaces. It is a versatile, cost-effective, and low-maintenance solution for any property owner looking to add elegance to their home or commercial space without the hefty price tag of real stone.

Benefits of lymestone

Versatility

lymestone is suitable for both interior and exterior applications, so it can be used to enhance the curb appeal of your home, as well as create stunning indoor living spaces.

Durability

Naturally strong, long-lasting, and weather-resistant, lymestone offers unmatched protection against cracks, chips, and fading.

Customization

Our experts can apply lymestone in various colors, textures, and patterns, ensuring it perfectly complements your existing architectural style.

Greater Sustainability

High in natural minerals and low in VOCs, lymestone ensures healthier living environments and a reduced carbon footprint for your property.

Complement Your Lymestone with Expert Foam Trim Services

To complete the stunning look of your lymestone project, One Stop Plastering also specializes in custom architectural foam trim services, providing you with all the essential design elements you need to elevate the beauty of your property’s exterior.

 

Our Foam Trim offerings include:

Window and Door Surrounds

We can create elegant designs that highlight your windows and doors while also ensuring enhanced insulation and weatherproofing.

Cornices and Soffits

Accentuate and protect your roofline with our intricate foam trim cornices and soffits, easily customizable to blend seamlessly with your existing architecture.

Column Wraps and Capitals

Add stately appeal to your property with our foam trim column wraps and capitals, designed to create the illusion of grand stone or wood columns without the heavy price tag and cumbersome installation.

Wall Caps and Top-offs

Protect and embellish your lymestone walls or veneers with our foam trim wall caps and top-offs, ensuring a polished and cohesive look throughout your entire property.

By combining lymestone and Foam Trim applications, we can bring your dream property to life, offering an aesthetic that is both timeless and durable.
